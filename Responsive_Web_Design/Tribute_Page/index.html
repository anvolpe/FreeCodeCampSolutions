Kobe Bryant

NBA player Kobe Bryant preparing to take a shot

Here's a time line of Kobe Bryant's life:



  • 1978 - Born in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania

  • 1991-1996 - Attends Lower Merion High School in Ardmore, Pennsylvania where he earns multiple highschool player of the year awards

  • 1996/1997 - wins the NBA Slam Dunk Contest and is named to the NBA All-Rookie Second Team

  • 1998 - starts NBA history at age 19

  • 2000-2002 - wins three NBA championships with the Lakers

  • 2003 - named to the All-NBA First Team

  • 2006 - scores 81 points against Toronto Raptors in a game

  • 2007 - youngest player in NBA history to gain 20.000 career points

  • 2008 - wins Most Valuable Player (MVP award)

  • 2009/2010 - leads Lakers to two more NBA championships

  • 2013 - becomes forth all-time leading scorer in NBA history

  • 2014 - becomes all-time leading scorer in Lakers franchise history

  • 2016 - plays his final NBA game

  • 2018 - wins Academy Award for Best Animated Short Film for "Dear Basketball"

  • 2018 - releases a book sharing insights from his basketball career

  • 2020 - passes away in a helicopter crash with his daughter Gianna and others
